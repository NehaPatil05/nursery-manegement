Fruit Name            : Mango

MangoKingdom    : Plantae

Family                    : Anacardiaceae

Order                      : Sapindales

Rank                       : Species

Common Name      : Mango

Description :




       Mango is a Strong fruit produce from numerous species of tropical trees belonging from flowering plant .When cultivated, the size, shape and branch density are determined by rootstock selection and trimming method.The leaves are alternately arranged green-colored simple ovals with serrated margins and slightly downy undersides.
       Mango Blossoms are produced in summer simultaneously with the budding of the leaves and are produced on spurs and some long shoots. The 3 to 4 cm (1 to 1 1⁄2 in) flowers are white with a pinktinge that gradually fades, five petaled, with an inflorescence consisting of a cyme with 4–6 flowers.The central flower of the inflorescence is called the "king bloom"; it opens first and can develop a larger fruit.
       fruit matures in late summer or autumn, and cultivars exist in a wide range of sizes.Commercial growers aim to produce an mango that is 7 to 8.5 cm (2 3⁄4 to 3 1⁄4 in) in diameter,due to market preference. Some consumers, especially those in Japan, prefer a larger mango, while mangos below 5.5 cm (2 1⁄4 in) are generally used for making juice and have little fresh market value.
